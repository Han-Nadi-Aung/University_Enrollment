What are FAQs?

How do I apply to Noble University?
It is an easy solution. Please go to"Course" tab in navigation bar and choose a course which a student want to join.Then,look for details. There is an enroll button and you can enroll it easily and we will respond back as soon as possible.

Are scholarships options available?
We highly value your interest in our scholarship program! To be considered for a full scholarship, we assess various criteria including excellent results in standardized tests such as IELTS, SAT Math, TOEFL, and PTE. While it's not mandatory to have outstanding scores in all of these tests, providing the relevant documentation showcasing your achievements will greatly enhance your application. We appreciate the effort you put into preparing and submitting these documents for our review.

How can I pay my tuition fees?
If you encounter difficulty in paying the full tuition fees upfront, we offer a convenient solution. You can opt to xsplit the payment into manageable installments. To be considered for this option, please ensure that you attach a detailed description of your financial circumstances and the proposed payment plan in your documentation file. We will carefully review your submission and take your situation into consideration during our evaluation process. Our aim is to make education accessible, and we appreciate your proactive approach in providing the necessary information for our assessment.

Are there networking events or alumni associations?
We take great pride in fostering a strong network among our alumni community. As part of our commitment to maintaining those connections, we organize regular networking events. These events include monthly webinars where alumni can come together either in-person or virtually. It provides a valuable opportunity to engage with fellow alumni, share insights, and expand professional networks. We encourage you to participate in these events, as they offer a platform for continued growth, collaboration, and the chance to stay connected with the Noble University community.
